# Network IOC Triage Guide

## Workflow
1. Validate IOC format and quality.
2. Enrich with reputation and context.
3. Correlate in SIEM, EDR, DNS, and firewall logs.
4. Classify confidence and take action.

## Actions
- High confidence malicious: block quickly.
- Suspicious: monitor and apply scoped controls.
- Unknown: continue enrichment.
