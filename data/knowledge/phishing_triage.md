# Phishing Triage Guide

## Triage Steps
- Validate sender, recipient set, and timing.
- Check SPF/DKIM/DMARC alignment.
- Inspect URLs and attachments in safe environment.

## Containment
- Remove messages from affected mailboxes.
- Block sender/domain/URL indicators.
- Reset credentials for impacted users.

## Escalate When
- Multiple users entered credentials.
- Malware execution is confirmed.
- Privileged accounts are targeted.
