# Containment Decision Matrix

## Decision Rules

- High confidence + high criticality: isolate immediately and escalate.
- High confidence + lower criticality: isolate and block indicators per SLA.
- Medium confidence + high criticality: apply scoped containment and expedited investigation.
- Low confidence: monitor and enrich before disruptive actions.

## Inputs
- Evidence confidence
- Asset criticality
- Business impact and blast radius

## Source/Reference

- Internal incident severity policy
- NIST incident handling practices
