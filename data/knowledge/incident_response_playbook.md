# Incident Response Playbook

## Severity
- Sev 1: Active compromise with major impact
- Sev 2: Confirmed malicious activity with limited scope
- Sev 3: Suspicious activity under investigation

## First 30 Minutes
- Validate alert quality and scope.
- Preserve key evidence.
- Assign incident commander and comms owner.

## Response Lifecycle
1. Identification
2. Containment
3. Eradication
4. Recovery
5. Lessons learned

- NIST SP 800-61
