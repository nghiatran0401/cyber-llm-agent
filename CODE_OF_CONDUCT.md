# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation respectful, inclusive, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Focus on technical merit and collaboration
- Provide constructive feedback
- Assume positive intent

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or hostile language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and participation that violate this policy.
